The giant square is perhaps the most crowded place Cat had ever seen. What seems like millions of people walk in all directions. Huge screens show advertisments and video clips. Suddenly, Cat runs into a movie crew. They are in the middle of the production for a new reality TV show. The producer, a glamorous lady dressed all in red, offers Cat the last empty spot on the show, promising him fame and eternal glory.
